Platform Features

  • Blockchain & NFTs for Skill Verification Certificates, credentials, and skills are stored as NFTs on the blockchain, ensuring authenticity and preventing fraud.
  • Decentralized Research Ownership Scientists and researchers can upload papers as NFTs, maintaining complete ownership and monetization rights.
  • AI-Powered EdTech Chatbot Recommends study materials, books, YouTube tutorials, and Wikipedia articles tailored to individual learning needs.
  • Smart Contracts for Publishing Rights Publishers can upload books and research papers with smart contracts, ensuring royalties and copyright protection.
  • AI-Driven Job & Skill Matching AI and ML algorithms analyze industry trends and recommend relevant jobs to students and professionals.
  • Web3 for Global Accessibility A decentralized approach allows users worldwide to access verified resources without censorship or centralized control.
